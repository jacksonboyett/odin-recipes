Steakhouse Burger

Juicy steakhouse burger

To cook the steakhouse burger you need beef, tomato, lettuce, cheese, and big buns.

You cook the beef and you put the hard beef on the big buns.

  • Cheese
  • Big buns

  2. Put the hard meat into the big buns
